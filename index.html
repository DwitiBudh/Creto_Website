See Solution View MVP

Problem Statement

Many artisans and creators struggle with limited digital presence, missing trust signals in online marketplaces, difficulty reaching local buyers, and lack of storytelling for their work.

Our Business Solution

CRETO is a trust-first handmade & creator marketplace that provides:

  • Escrow-based safe payments
  • Local discovery to find nearby creators
  • Storytelling profiles with photos & videos (MTM — Meet The Maker)
  • Custom product requests & live stream shopping

Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

  • Trust & safety features (escrow, verification)
  • Local discovery system
  • Emotional creator story profiles
  • Follow creators, request custom products, and live shopping

Core Features

Escrow Payments

Payments are held until the buyer confirms delivery — protects both parties.

Local Discovery

Search by city, neighbourhood or nearby artisans to reduce logistics and build community.

MTM — Meet The Maker

Story pages with videos and photos to build emotional connection.

Custom Requests & Live Sales

Buyers request custom handmade items or purchase during creator live streams.

Business Plan

  • Revenue: Platform fee of 5–8% on each sale.
  • Year 1: Onboard creators & build trust.
  • Year 2: Expand into Tier-2/Tier-3 cities and add premium services.
  • Scalability: Low ops costs, replicable to neighbouring countries.

Go-to-Market Strategy

  • Invite creators from Instagram & Facebook to join early.
  • Run social ads, creator partnerships & "Support Local Creators" campaigns.
  • Go hyperlocal with college fests, flea markets & local art fairs.
  • Drive referral growth — reward both referrer and referee.

Competitive Landscape

CRETO differentiates via a trust-first escrow model, hyperlocal discovery, and deep storytelling for makers.

  • AuthIndia — Easy onboarding
  • Okhai — NGO & women-focused
  • Jaypore — Premium curated marketplace
  • Etsy (India) — Global but difficult for small sellers
  • India Craft House, iTokri — regional players
